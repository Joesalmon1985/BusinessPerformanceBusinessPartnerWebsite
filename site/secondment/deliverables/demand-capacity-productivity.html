This is a weekly operational view for service managers: how much demand is coming in, how much capacity we have, how long people are waiting, and how productive teams are. It is designed for running services day to day — separate from national mandatory returns. It helps distinguish real pressure on the ground from numbers that changed because of the IT migration.
