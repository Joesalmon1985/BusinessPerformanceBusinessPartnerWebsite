In plain English

This is a route map from the ward or clinic to the board report. For every figure that appears in a report, it shows where that number comes from in the clinical systems, who owns it, and whether we trust it right now. It also lists which reports exist, who they are for, and which business questions they answer. If a figure looks wrong, you can trace it back without needing to be a data specialist.
