Answering the interview question — approach

This is a 6 month secondment — what do I hope to achieve in those 6 months, and what would my approach be to ensure I am successful?

The achieve half is the six handover deliverables and performance infrastructure. The approach half is three expedition principles applied to Band 8a performance partnering:

My approach would be disciplined, evidence-led and resilient: Fanatic Discipline to keep the work moving, Test and Scale to validate before committing, and Productive Paranoia to protect reporting confidence during change.

Three principles — one page each

Fanatic Discipline

Steady, repeatable progress — ten miles every day, not zero when conditions are hard.

Supports the plan: agreed definitions, validation rhythm, ownership and handover.

Test and Scale

Test routes cheaply with probes and pilots; scale decisively once evidence is clear.

Supports the plan: definition tests, prototypes, promoted deliverables, dismissed bad ideas.

Productive Paranoia

Prepared vigilance when dashboards refresh but definitions may have drifted.
