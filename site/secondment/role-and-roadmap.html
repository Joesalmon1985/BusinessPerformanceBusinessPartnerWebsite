What I hope to achieve

By month six: agreed definitions register, source-to-report map, risk register, report catalogue, validation and reconciliation outputs, confidence model, demand/capacity insight, senior brief template, improvement tracker, handover documentation, and a reusable model for future change.

My approach to succeed

Listen first, then define, control migration reporting, prototype outputs, validate and embed, hand over and sustain — working across operational services, BI, IM&T, finance, workforce and senior leadership as a bridge, not a solo technical fixer.
