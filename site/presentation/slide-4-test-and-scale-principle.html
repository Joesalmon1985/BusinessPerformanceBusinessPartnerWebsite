Speaker notes

Test and Scale is the antidote to embedding untested definitions in board packs and operational meetings. In transition, new data models and pipelines invite optimism — “it’s live, so it must be right.” The principle is to test mappings, inclusion rules and product meaning on a small scale first, then scale what survives evidence.

What this slide delivers — plain English

Test and Scale
Second operating principle: try small, cheap tests before embedding definitions in board packs, mandatory returns or service contracts. B&P designs the test, gathers evidence and recommends promote, park or stop.
Test cheaply
Shadow runs, two-team pilots, side-by-side compares — low cost before organisation-wide commitment. Avoids “it’s live in Epic so we must trust it.”
Then commit
Scale only when evidence shows managers can use the product without BI translation and definitions match agreed rules. B&P documents the decision in the ideas register or deliverable status.
