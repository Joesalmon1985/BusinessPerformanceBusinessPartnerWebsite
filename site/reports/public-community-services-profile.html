Worked example: AI-assisted CSDS community activity briefing

CSDS community activity profile — RDY, Mar 2026 with descriptive trend

Public-data demonstration report: This is not an official Dorset HealthCare report. It uses public aggregate data only and requires human review and local owner confirmation before operational use.

What the agent was asked to do

Business question: Using public CSDS for Mar 2026 , explain community activity measures, show descriptive six-month trends where the historic stack supports them, and what a business partner should validate locally.

Dataset: CSDS Monthly Statistics (RDY provider rows)

This is a first-draft analytical brief for human review — not an approved performance report.

Scope: Provider / RDY rows only — not Dorset population or ICB-resident views.

Data used

  • CSDS (demo_csds_activity.csv — Mar 2026)
  • trend_csds_activity_rdy.csv — historic CareActivities stack
  • Period covered: Oct 2025 – Mar 2026 (six-month trend); latest month Mar 2026
  • RDY filter: ODS code RDY; Dorset HealthCare trust name variants (see filter notes)
  • Historic trend data: Yes — six-month historic stack (6 months in trend_csds_activity_rdy.csv)

What this report can and cannot tell us

This report can:

  • Describe Mar 2026 community activity totals and key activity types for RDY.
  • Show descriptive six-month trends for Assessment and Clinical Intervention where the historic stack supports them.

This report cannot:

  • Prove referral demand, waiting times or team performance from public aggregate CSDS.
  • Support pathway or team-level conclusions without local drill-down.

For that reason, this should be treated as a first-draft prompt for review, not a final performance judgement.

Headline reading

  • Both Assessment and Clinical Intervention increased in Mar 2026 compared with the previous month, but both remain lower than the start of the six-month window (Assessment -29%, Clinical Intervention -8.2%). Local validation needed.
  • 'Other' is the largest activity category (9,995, 42% of total) — limits interpretability.
  • Community waiting-time performance requires CHS SitRep / NHS waiting-list data — not CSDS activity counts. <cite class="nhs-standard-cite"><a href="https://www.england.nhs.uk/statistics/statistical-work-areas/community-health-services-waiting-lists/" rel="noopener">NHS England community health services waiting lists; published 2025/26 planning guidance; accessed 2026-06-21; confidence: confirmed</a></cite>
  • Public aggregate CSDS activity counts cannot prove referral demand, waiting times or team performance.

Priority review flags

  • 'Other' accounts for 42% of ActivityType activities (9,995) — major data-quality/interpretability flag.
  • Assessment and Clinical Intervention rose month-on-month but remain below six-month start values.

Why this is useful

  • The agent extracted RDY CSDS activity-type counts and computed descriptive six-month trends where the historic stack supports them.
  • It flagged where activity data cannot answer access or waiting questions, and where coding limits interpretation.

Human validation checklist

  • Is this the same definition used locally? — Public datasets may use national definitions. Local dashboards may use different filters, denominators or reporting dates.
  • Is this still the latest position? — Public data may be delayed. Local operational data may have moved on since the reporting period shown.
  • Is the figure affected by data quality, suppression, coding or service model? — A public figure can be affected by small numbers, exclusions, coding rules or how services are configured.
  • Who owns the local narrative? — The accountable service, finance, workforce, quality or BI owner should confirm the explanation before use.
  • Is this suitable for board/service reporting, or only a prompt for further review? — These briefs are first drafts — confirm whether the figure is ready for formal reporting or needs more local work.

Bottom line

This is an activity profile, not a community services performance report. March activity rose for key types but six-month movement is down for Assessment and Clinical Intervention. Waiting-time performance needs separate CHS waiting-list data. A community services lead should validate coding, especially the large 'Other' category, before any operational use.
