Mandatory reporting map

A proof-of-concept mandatory reporting overview — the kind of visibility a Business & Performance Business Partner would want — demonstrating how agentic AI could help identify, organise and maintain reporting requirements.

Demonstration caveat: This is a personal demonstration site. It is not an official Dorset HealthCare register, is not complete, and is not operationally validated. It does not contain confidential or patient-identifiable information.
Illustrative metadata: Owners, due dates, assurance statuses and local source labels are synthetic or partly fabricated for demonstration. Reference links marked Pub point to public NHS sources. An agent could help draft and maintain this register; humans must validate against local SOPs and named owners before any operational use.

How agentic AI could help

  • Extract reporting requirements from public specifications (MHSDS, PLCM, ADC, etc.)
  • Propose owner, cadence and source mappings for human confirmation
  • Flag gaps, conflicting cadences and missing documentation links
  • Maintain a change log when national specifications update
  • Cross-reference the public source catalogue in public-data/DATA_SOURCE_REGISTER.csv

What this register maps

  • Return name, owner, frequency and next due date (demo)
  • Assurance status, risk and brief issues / notes
  • Public reference link — verified working URLs only

Additional detail (recipient, mandate, source system, confidence) is held in data/mandatory_returns_register.csv. Returns without a verified public reference are omitted from this demo table for now.

See the R-generated Mandatory Returns Assurance Log for an operational assurance view with escalation routes.
